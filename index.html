About me
Hey! I'm Mariano, an Engineer based in London, UK.

After learning the ropes mixing bands in Boston, I'm now back in London and ready to mix your songs!

I'm a musician myself, so I know how important it is to get the right sound for your songs. I'll work with you to make sure you get the sound you want.

I understand time is money! Expect fast turnaround times.

I've completed my mixing studies at the renowned Berklee College of Music with the highest grade. Berklee is known as the world's foremost institute for the study of jazz and modern American music (quote).

Whilst at Berklee, I've worked under the mentoring of Mixing Engineer Dave Dysart, who has mixed Yes' album Heaven and Earth.
